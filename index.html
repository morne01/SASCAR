DECIDE, COMMIT, SUCCEED

What we do

We feed poverty-stricken children and their families. We rescue children from seperated family members due to unfortunate reasons.

Our main goal

Our main goal is to establish a SASCAR branch in every province to reach as many children in need as possible.

Where are we involved

We are massively involved in rescueing refugees and bringing them to South Africa for rehabilitaion and and giving them a new hope in their lives

Ownership

SASCAR Organisastion was established in 2021 and is owned by Triakshan. We are planning to establish more of the SASCAR branches in each province
